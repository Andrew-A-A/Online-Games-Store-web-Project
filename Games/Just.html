Just Cause™ 3

Description :

The Mediterranean republic of Medici is suffering under the brutal control of General Di Ravello, a dictator with an insatiable appetite for power.
Enter Rico Rodriguez, a man on a mission to destroy the General’s hold on power by any means necessary.
With over 400 square miles of complete freedom from sky to seabed and a huge arsenal of weaponry, gadgets and vehicles, prepare to unleash chaos in the most creative and explosive ways you can imagine.
